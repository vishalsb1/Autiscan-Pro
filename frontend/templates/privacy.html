Privacy & Data Protection

Your privacy is our top priority

Complete Privacy Guarantee

What We DON'T Do

  • NO DATA STORAGE - Your responses are never saved to any database
  • NO TRACKING - We don't track your identity or create user profiles
  • NO SHARING - Your information is never shared with third parties
  • NO COOKIES - No persistent cookies or tracking technologies
  • NO LOGS - Personal responses are not logged or recorded

How Our Assessment Works

1
You complete the assessment form
2
Your responses are processed in memory only
3
Results are calculated and displayed immediately
4
All data is immediately discarded when you leave

Technical Implementation

Our application is designed with privacy by design:

  • Stateless Processing: No server-side data persistence
  • Memory-Only: Calculations performed in temporary memory
  • Session-Based: Temporary session IDs only (discarded on exit)
  • No Database: No backend database to store personal information
  • Local Processing: All calculations happen on our server temporarily

Security Measures

  • HTTPS Encryption: All communication is encrypted in transit
  • No Data Retention: Zero data retention policy
  • Minimal Data Collection: Only essential information for calculation
  • Anonymous Processing: No identifying information required

Legal Compliance

Our privacy approach ensures compliance with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Medical Ethics Guidelines for screening tools

Additional Privacy Recommendations

  • Use a private/incognito browser window for extra privacy
  • Clear your browser cache after using the tool (though not necessary)
  • Take a screenshot of your results if you want to keep them
  • Discuss results with healthcare professionals in person, not via email

Questions About Privacy?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us. We're committed to transparency and protecting your privacy.
